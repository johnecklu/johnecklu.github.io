About Axil Technologies

Introduction

As browser capabilities improve, users have less and less patience for anything.. "behind the times". If you want to hold their attention for longer than a few seconds, you have to keep your interfaces looking sharp and up-to-date with modern technology and a relevant design. it's a lot to keep up with, but that's where AxilMedia and our front-end team can help.

We use our expertise in HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript to create beautiful, intuiive, interactive experience that will keep your users coming back for more. Mean while, the backend team makes sure your new stuff integrates seamlessly with propriety APIs, legacy database, and any other techno-bogeymen reckless enough to challenge us.

Services

Real Worl Web

Webapps are a pretty big deal to us. When we commit to building you one, we focus on the solution using the latest cools available. To reduce the cost of implementation and delivery, we manage the project implementation / ownership and to allow the projects to be efficiently maintained after we hand over the keys.

Responsive UX Designs

Responsive design is more than a trend; it's a requirement. Nowadays, targeting iPhone, iPad, and Adroid means supporting at least 12 deifferent resolutions. Not to mention that most of these devices can be flipped into landscape and protrait mode. But responsive implementation and design are relatively new skills sets, and many product/ marketing teams don't have relevant expertise inhouse.. yet. AxilMedia specializes in responsive design. We focus on two critical ares: 1. Reducing implementation risks and 2. setting up fron-end code you can maintain without us.
